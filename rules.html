While our in-game environment favors absolute sandbox survival, our community platforms adhere to standard guidelines to maintain operational integrity.

Community Code of Conduct
01. Organized Communication

Kindly direct conversations to their designated topics. If you require assistance regarding specific game directories, reach out via the official help desk rather than using general channels.
02. Mutual Respect

Targeted hostility, personal degradation, and constructive harassment toward community members or groups are completely prohibited across all common platforms.
03. Chat Flow Maintenance

Please refrain from flooding chat channels. This includes avoiding large blocks of text, excessive capitalization, message repetition, or spamming media files.
04. Moderation Directives

Respect choices made by administrative coordinators. Disputes regarding formal platform rules should be handled through appropriate support tickets.
05. Confidentiality Protocol

Distributing, collecting, or threatening to expose private real-world personal information belonging to any individual will result in an immediate network restriction.
06. Profile Appropriateness

Account identities, aliases, custom avatars, and status messages must remain clear, respectful, and fully compliant with clean community environments.
Administrative Guidelines & Frameworks
Enforcement Measures
  1. Initial Notice: Formal record documentation.
  2. Temporary Mute: Communication limitations across network assets.
  3. Account Suspension: Temporary removal of platform entry credentials.
  4. Network Ban: Definitive hardware and infrastructure restriction.
Internal Directives (Staff)
  • Operator Limits: Default root level console access is fully contained.
  • Standard Physics: Administrative characters maintain standard gameplay parameters.
  • Directory Security: Staff accounts are restricted from accessing external index channels.
