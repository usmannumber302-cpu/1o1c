📜 1O1C Rules

By participating in this server, you agree to follow all rules laid out below. Let's keep the community functional and welcoming for everyone.

1. Use Appropriate Channels
Keep discussions relevant to their designated channels. If a game does not have its own channel, notify staff instead of posting elsewhere.
2. No Harassment
Any form of harassment, bullying, or targeted toxicity against others will not be tolerated.
3. No Spamming
Avoid excessive or repetitive messages, including text, emojis, capital letters, or media files in discord or in-game chats.
4. Respect Staff and Rules
Follow all instructions from staff members. Arguing, wasting time, or ignoring moderation decisions is strictly prohibited.
5. No Doxxing
Sharing or threatening to share personal or private real-life information about anyone will result in an immediate blacklisting.
6. Profile Restrictions
Profile pictures and usernames/nicknames must remain appropriate, clean, and free of explicit or sexually suggestive material.
7. No Rule Exploitation
Attempting to bypass or exploit technical loopholes in these rules is not allowed. Report any system issues directly to staff.
8. No NSFW Content
Posting or sharing any Not Safe For Work material is completely forbidden.
9. No Racism or Discrimination
Hate speech or discrimination based on race, identity, ethnicity, gender, or religion will be met with immediate expulsion.
10. No Self-Promotion
Advertising external Discord links, servers, or unapproved media content is strictly banned.

⚖️ Enforcement & Punishments

  • 1st Offense: Formal Warning
  • 2nd Offense: Temporary System Mute
  • 3rd Offense: Temporary Network Ban
  • Severe Violations: Immediate, permanent ban (Doxxing, Hate Speech, Explicit/NSFW content).

Note: Punishments escalate automatically based on severity. Staff decisions are final.

⚙️ System Security Core (Staff Directives)

  • NO /op: Default operator assignments are locked down.
  • NO GOD MODE IN-GAME: Survival mechanics apply universally across standard parameters.
  • EXTERNAL NETWORK RESTRICTION: No admin or staff member will connect to an external server unless it is explicitly designated on our trusted verification index.
  • COMMAND PROTOCOL: Do not execute actions you have been explicitly told not to perform. Integrity is mandatory.
